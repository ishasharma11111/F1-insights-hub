About F1 Insights Hub

Welcome to F1 Insights Hub — a fan-made hub built to bring the excitement of Formula 1 to one neat, interactive place. Whether you want driver stats, circuit info, quick games, or bite-sized insights, this site puts it all together with a modern, mobile-friendly design.

What you'll find here

  • Drivers: Updated 2025 driver line-ups, profiles, and key stats.
  • Teams: Visual team overviews with car liveries on the homepage.
  • Circuits: Track facts, lengths, laps and fun notes for each circuit.
  • Fun Zone: Quizzes, reaction tests and mini-games to test your F1 knowledge.
  • Lightweight & fast: Image-optimized and responsive for phones and desktops.

Our mission

To make Formula 1 more approachable and fun — whether you're a longtime fan or just getting started. We build simple, interactive features that highlight what makes F1 thrilling: speed, strategy, and human skill.

Want to help?
